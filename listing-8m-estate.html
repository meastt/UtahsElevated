The Residence

Where Desert Meets Distinction

This stunning new construction tri-level estate at 936 E Sapphire Sky Ln represents an extraordinary opportunity to own one of St. George's most impressive residences, set to be completed in 2025.

Spanning 10,984 square feet across three levels, this architectural masterpiece features 8 bedrooms and 9 bathrooms with vaulted ceilings, a dedicated theater room, and smart home technology throughout.
